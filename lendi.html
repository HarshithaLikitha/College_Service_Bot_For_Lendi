About Lendi Institute

Lendi Institute of Engineering & Technology is a premier engineering institution established in 2008. Located near Vizag, Lendi provides a serene campus, experienced faculty and industry-linked programs to prepare students for global careers in engineering and technology.

  • Accredited programs & autonomous status
  • UG & PG programs in Engineering
  • Strong placement support and industry partnerships
Know more

Vision

Producing globally competent and quality technocrats with human values for the holistic needs of industry and society.

Mission

Provide strong infrastructure, research culture and industry-relevant education to prepare students for employment, higher studies and entrepreneurship.
